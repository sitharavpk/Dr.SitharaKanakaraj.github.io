About Me

I am Sithara Kanakaraj, a professional in transition from the realm of academia, where I served as an Assistant Professor, to the dynamic domain of Data Analysis. I bring with me a wealth of knowledge and expertise, backed by a distinguished academic journey. I hold a doctoral degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the esteemed National Institute of Technology Calicut, India.

Since I was young, I have always enjoyed solving puzzles and identifying patterns & trends in the things around me. I remember my parents saying that I have been very meticulous and dedicated in doing my activities right from school days. I look at data in a similar way : to me it is one big puzzle that has to be inspected in detail to derive patterns nobody else sees.

I have been working as an Assistant Professor for four years and also have a research experience of more than ten years. My passion for turning data into actionable strategies extends beyond the classroom. I am proficient in programming languages like Python and RStudio, as well as data analysis tools such as SQL , Excel, and data visualization libraries like Matplotlib and Tableau.
